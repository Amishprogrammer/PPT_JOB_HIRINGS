The Referral Advantage

How Data-Driven Referral Programs Revolutionize Hiring, Retention, and Your Bottom Line

Presenter: Amish Singh

The Hiring Problem: Slow, Expensive, and Ineffective

Traditional hiring is time-consuming, often taking 39 to 55 days to fill a role.

High costs associated with job boards, advertising, and long screening processes.

Poor retention rates, especially from job boards (only 14% of hires stay for two years).

Finding candidates who are a great cultural fit is a major challenge.

The Solution is Already in Your Building

Your own employees are your best source for high-quality, long-lasting talent.

Employee referral programs leverage your team's trusted networks to find candidates who are pre-vetted for skill and cultural fit.

Referrals Deliver Quality, Not Just Quantity

40% of Hires

from Referrals

Key Takeaway:

An applicant from a referral is far more likely to be hired than one from any other source.

Why This Matters

Data-Backed: The statistics overwhelmingly prove that referrals are the most effective hiring source.
Better ROI: Lower hiring costs and higher retention lead to significant long-term savings.
Stronger Culture: Hiring people who are already connected to your team builds a more cohesive and engaged workforce.
Viable Business: The technology costs are manageable, and the service provides clear value to colleges, making it a strong business case.
